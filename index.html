About

I’m a Level 6 Creative Computing student with a strong focus on designing and developing interactive digital experiences. My work explores areas such as procedural visual generation, creative coding, and web-based applications, often combining technical implementation with artistic experimentation. I enjoy building projects that are both visually engaging and functionally dynamic, using modern tools and frameworks to bring ideas to life. Alongside development, I actively document my creative and technical process, sharing progress updates, breakdowns, and detailed walkthroughs on my YouTube channel. This not only helps me reflect on my work but also allows others to learn from my approach and techniques. You can find direct links to these videos on the project cards, where each piece is accompanied by its corresponding documentation and insights.
